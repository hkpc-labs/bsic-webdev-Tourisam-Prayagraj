Triveni Sangam

The sacred confluence of the Ganga, Yamuna, and Saraswati rivers. Pilgrims flock here for holy dips.

Allahabad Fort

Perched on the banks of the Yamuna, this fort is a blend of Persian and Mughal architecture, built by Emperor Akbar.

Allahabad Museum

Located in Chandrashekhar Park, this museum houses a vast collection of artifacts, including terracotta from Kaushambi.

Khusro Bagh

An exquisite Mughal garden, Khusro Bagh is the resting place of Prince Khusro, the eldest son of Emperor Jahangir.
